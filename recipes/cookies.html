Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic staple dessert in many households. Enjoy this simple recipe to make absolutely delectable cookies, that will impress everyone!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup (142g) light brown sugar, packed
  • 2/3 cup (131g) granulated sugar
  • 8 tablespoons (113g) unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup (92g) vegetable shortening
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt (use 1/2 teaspoon salt if you use salted butter)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract, optional
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups (240g) King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips
